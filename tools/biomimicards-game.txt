<h1>Overview</h1>
<p><a href="https://circulab.academy/circular-economy-tools/biomimicards/">Biomimicards game</a> is an educational tool designed to introduce and explore the concept of biomimicry. It consists of a deck of cards, each featuring an illustration of a natural element and its corresponding technological innovation inspired by nature's strategies. The game aims to foster creativity and innovation by encouraging players to consider how natural solutions can address human challenges. It is suitable for a diverse audience, from students to professionals, and can be played individually or in groups. As part of the Circulab toolbox, Biomimicards also supports the transition towards a circular and regenerative economy.</p>
<hr>
<h1>Dimensions</h1>
<p><strong>Resource Type</strong>
Biomimicards is an engaging and interactive tool designed to facilitate learning and ideation in relation to biomimicry and sustainability.</p>

</ul>
<p><strong>Objective</strong>
The primary goal of Biomimicards is to educate and inspire new ideas by showcasing how nature's solutions can be translated into human innovation.</p>

<li>Tags: #educate, #inspire, #assess</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Target Audience</strong>
Biomimicards is versatile and can be utilized by a range of users, from educational institutions to businesses. However, it must be seen as an educational aid that looks in raising awareness.</p>

</ul>

This game emphasizes the environmental aspect of sustainability by demonstrating how natural mechanisms can lead to sustainable technological advancements.</p>

</ul>
<p><strong>Innovation Type</strong>
