Overview

Biomimicards game is an educational tool designed to introduce and explore the concept of biomimicry. It consists of a deck of cards, each featuring an illustration of a natural element and its corresponding technological innovation inspired by nature's strategies. The game aims to foster creativity and innovation by encouraging players to consider how natural solutions can address human challenges. It is suitable for a diverse audience, from students to professionals, and can be played individually or in groups. As part of the Circulab toolbox, Biomimicards also supports the transition towards a circular and regenerative economy.
