Overview

The Social Impact Wheel is a strategic framework designed to help organizations assess and enhance their contribution to social responsibility, particularly in alignment with the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). It serves as a reflective instrument for companies to evaluate their innovation strategies and their broader impact on society and the environment. By employing this tool, organizations can identify their strengths, areas for improvement, and prioritize their impact values. It is a proactive measure to safeguard reputations and ensure that innovations are not only profitable but also socially and environmentally sustainable, thereby future-proofing business practices.
