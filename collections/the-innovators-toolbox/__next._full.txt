f:T72e,<p><a href="https://www.boardofinnovation.com/tools/">Board of Innovation</a> offers several business related tools that primarily focus on the financial aspects of innovating and entrepreneuring. A few of the canvases stick out as they relate to social impact of such activities.</p>
<h1>Social Impact Toolkit</h1>
<p>First one is the <a href="https://www.boardofinnovation.com/tools/social-impact-toolkit/">Social Impact Toolkit</a>. This toolkit is created for nonprofits and businesses that want to create partnerships that drive change. It helps by directing the user to get an overview of the 17 UN Global Goals and find out what the related targets are. An interactive set of tools and frameworks can be found <a href="https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVOxFPzhg=/">here</a>.</p>
<p><img src="/tools/pasted-image-20240301155443png" alt="Pasted image 20240301155443.png"></p>
<h1>Social Partnership Flowcharts</h1>
<p>The second on is <a href="https://www.boardofinnovation.com/tools/social-impact-partnerships-flowchart/">Social Partnership Flowcharts</a>. It helps to see what partnership type is the most suitable depending on the contextual circumstances of the activity. An interactive canvas can be found <a href="https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVOxSMJr0=/">here</a>.</p>
<p><img src="/tools/pasted-image-20240301155631png" alt="Pasted image 20240301155631.png"></p>
<h1>Social Impact Intentions Mapper</h1>
<p>And, the third one is <a href="https://www.boardofinnovation.com/tools/social-impact-intentions-mapper/">Social Impact Intentions Mapper</a>. It helps to determine what the impact of what one is trying to make is and what approach is taken. An interactive canvas can be found <a href="https://miro.com/app/board/uXjVOxSMJr0=/">here</a>.</p>
