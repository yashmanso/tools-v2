Tools for Design and Sustainability

VentureWell gathers several tools and frameworks in one place where they offer ways for engineers and designers to monitor their impact. They also provide guidance how to integrate them into classrooms.

The website structures them in several categories. Each category is accompanied by some knowledge nuggets and videos that show case tutorials and practical examples:

  • **Whole System Mapping
  • **Measuring Sustainability
  • **Greener Materials
  • **Lightweighting
  • **Circular Economy
  • **Energy Effectiveness
  • **Changing Lifestyles
  • **Biomimicry
