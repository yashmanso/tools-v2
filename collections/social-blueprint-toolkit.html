Social Blueprint Toolkit

Social Blueprint Toolkit is developed and supported by Impact Strategy Advisors and largely focuses on the social impacts of businesses. Derived from the practice based knowledge, these tools are offered in an effort to help build and advance the value driven business.  Each tool addresses the nuances of building operating companies, programs, and investment funds in the new purpose economy.

The toolkit includes the following tools and canvases as well as guides:

  • Social Blueprint Impact Canvas
  • Social Blueprint Impact Optimization Quad
  • Social Blueprint Business Profile Framework
  • Social Lean Curriculum Approach
  • Social Blueprint Impact Judge Notepad
