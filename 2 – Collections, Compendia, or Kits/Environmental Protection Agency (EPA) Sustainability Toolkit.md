
[E3 (Economy, Energy and Environment)](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#sustainable) unit at the EPA has assembled more than 60 tools and resources to help participants, stakeholders, and assessment teams address various area of improvement. The tools are separated into the following buckets:

-   [**Sustainable Manufacturing Tools**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#sustainable)
-   [**Life Cycle Assessment Tools**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#lca)
-   [**Energy Efficiency Tools**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#energy)
-   [**Carbon Footprint Tools**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#carbon)
-   [**Materials Management: Toxic Chemicals and Pollution Prevention Tools**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#materials)
-   [**Community Development Tools**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#community)
-   [**Worker Safety Tools**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#safety)
-   [**Workforce Development Tools**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#workforce)
-   [**Funding and Other Resources**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#funding)
-   [**Manufacturing Industry Resources**](https://www.epa.gov/e3/e3-sustainability-tools#industry)
